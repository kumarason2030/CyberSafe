Essential Password Security Tips

1. Length Matters

Use passwords that are at least 12 characters long. Longer passwords are harder to crack.

Weak: cat123

Strong: WhiskersPurr2024!

2. Mix Different Characters

Include a combination of:

  • Uppercase letters (A-Z)
  • Lowercase letters (a-z)
  • Numbers (0-9)
  • Special characters (!@#$%^&*)

3. Avoid Personal Information

Don't use:

  • Your name or birthday
  • Family members' names
  • Pet names
  • Common words from the dictionary

4. Use Unique Passwords

Never reuse passwords across different accounts. Each account should have a unique password.

5. Regular Updates

Change your passwords every 3-6 months, especially for critical accounts like banking and email.
