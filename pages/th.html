Remember

Cybercriminals are constantly developing new types of scams. While this guide helps you understand common threats, your best defense is always:

  • Trust your instincts
  • If something seems too good to be true, it probably is
  • Never share sensitive information under pressure
  • When in doubt, verify independently through official channels
  • Report suspicious activities immediately
